What I have done

What I have done:

Developed a predictive model to forecast sales revenue for a retail company using machine learning algorithms such as Random Forest, XGBoost, and Neural Networks. The model achieved an accuracy of 90% and helped the company optimize their inventory and increase their revenue.

Conducted a comprehensive analysis of customer behavior for an e-commerce company, which included segmentation, cohort analysis, and customer lifetime value calculation. The insights gained from the analysis helped the company tailor their marketing strategy and increase customer retention.

Designed and implemented a dashboard to monitor key performance indicators (KPIs) for a SaaS company. The dashboard provided real-time insights into user behavior, product usage, and revenue, which enabled the company to make data-driven decisions and improve their product offerings.

Conducted a sentiment analysis on social media data for a consumer goods company to understand customer opinions about their products. The analysis helped the company identify areas for improvement and develop a more customer-centric approach to product development.

Developed a recommendation engine for a media company using collaborative filtering algorithms. The engine provided personalized recommendations to users based on their viewing history and preferences, which increased user engagement and retention.

Conducted a regression analysis to identify factors that influence customer churn for a telecom company. The analysis helped the company develop a targeted retention strategy and reduce customer churn.

These are just a few examples of the projects I have worked on in data analysis and data science. I am constantly seeking new challenges and opportunities to apply my skills and expertise to real-world problems.
